Professional CAD Drafting Services


Serving various industries nationwide. Our services includes telecom layout design for new underground or overhead fiber optic path and connectivity, layout for repair and maintenance of underground conduits and manholes, ground beds and pole anchors layout, site specific traffic control plan design. We also provide stone shop drawing services in CAD plans, elevations & details of presentations. Our professional team is dedicated to deliver excellent customer service.




Telecom Outside Plant Layout

involves the design and installation of physical infrastructure, such as cables, conduits, and poles, to connect telecommunications services from a central office to customers. It includes planning the routes, securing right-of-way, and ensuring reliable, high-quality connections in diverse environmental conditions.


Traffic Control Plan

Outlines the strategies and measures to safely manage vehicle and pedestrian movement through or around construction zones, events, or other temporary disruptions on roadways. It includes signage, barriers, and detour routes to ensure safety and minimize traffic disruption.


Architectural Stone Shop Drawings

Detailed, precise plans that illustrate the dimensions, placement, and construction details of stone elements in a building project. These drawings serve as a guide for fabricators and installers, ensuring that the stone components fit correctly and meet the design specifications.


Electrical Design

Involves the planning and specification of electrical systems in buildings or infrastructure, including power distribution, lighting, communication networks, and control systems. It ensures that electrical components are safely and efficiently integrated, meeting both functional requirements and regulatory standards.
