  Logos Engineering and Design

We specialize in telecom layout design, including new fiber optic paths, repairs, and ground infrastructure. Our services also extend to stone shop drawings and traffic control plans. We’re committed to providing excellent customer service nationwide.

Services Offered

Telecom Outside Plant Layout

Involves the design and installation of physical infrastructure, such as cables, conduits, and poles, to connect telecommunications services from a central office to customers.


Traffic Control Plan

Outlines the strategies and measures to safely manage vehicle and pedestrian movement through or around construction zones, events, or other temporary disruptions on roadways.


Architectural Stone Shop Drawings

Detailed, precise plans that illustrate the dimensions, placement, and construction details of stone elements in a building project.


Architectural Drafting

Architectural drafting is the process of creating detailed drawings of buildings and structures to communicate design ideas and construction requirements.
